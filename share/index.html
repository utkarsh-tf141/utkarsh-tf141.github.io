About ShARE

  • ShARE is an innovative start-up at the crossroads of education and consulting with ‘Do Well, Do Good’ as its mission. ShARE aims to transform the way corporate organizations conventionally work and create a new generation of leaders & changemakers who will do well for themselves and do good for the whole world.
  • The founder of ShARE Sebesatian Frendo identified that there was a gap between the student and corporate life and caused a delay of a couple of years to acquire the necessary skills. Hence why not train the students on the necessary skills in their academic life instead. That’s how this journey began in 2002 and has evolved a lot since then. What started as a Think Tank to facilitate the dynamic exchange of ideas on global issues among students has evolved into a prestigious Leadership Programme Visit the official website of ShARE to know more: https://www.share-share.org/
